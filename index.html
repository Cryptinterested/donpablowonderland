Frequently Asked Questions

WHAT IS IT ABOUT?

The Don Pablo Wonderland is a place without rules dedicated to art for collectibles, unique works, cartoon series and graffiti. The common point of these works is that they all come from real street art. Don Pablo is the ambassador of this world, created between real life, street art and NFT. Each piece is unique and handmade on the street.

THE MOTTO?

No rules, just art.

Where can I find it?

The NFT Don Pablo will soon be available on the Tezos Objkt.com marketplace platform. You will need to have a few tez (XTZ) in your wallet to hit them.

How many Don Pablo will be minted?

A first set of 30 Don Pablo will be available soon. Many more collectibles will be available soon. A cartoon series will be developed from the funds generated by NFT sales.
