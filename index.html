Frequently Asked Questions

WHAT IS IT ABOUT?

The Don Pablo Wonderland is a place without rules dedicated to art for collectibles, unique works, cartoon series and graffiti. The common point of these artworks is that they all come from real street art. Don Pablo is the ambassador of this fantasy world, created between real life, street art and NFTs. Each piece is unique and handmade.

THE MOTTO?

No rules, just Art.

Where can I find it?

The NFT Don Pablo will soon be available on the Tezos objkt.com marketplace. You will need to have a few tez (XTZ) in your wallet to snap them up.

How many Don Pablo will be minted?

A first set of 30 Don Pablo will be available soon, and many more collectibles will be available later on. A cartoon series will be developed from the funding generated by selling the NFTs. One character per episode will be created in 20 NFT collectibles and NFT owners will have the opportunity to win some unique characters in a lottery.

Where can the Don Pablo be traded?

All Don Pablo Wonderland NFTs will be available exclusively on the Tezos blockchain. You will be able to sell your Don Pablo on the objkt.com secondary market.

Why should I own a Don Pablo NFT?

Don Pablo Wonderland is an NFT project that connects NFTs and real life by creating collectibles from street art and graffiti. By becoming an NFT owner, you will participate in the development of the very first cartoon series using NFTs and street art. You will discover many staged characters around unique and funny stories.
